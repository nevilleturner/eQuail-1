Our Process: How Your E-Waste Finds New Life

1. Safe E-Waste Collection

Equail organizes convenient collection drives and offers dedicated drop-off points throughout Jeddah and surrounding areas. Whether you have old phones, computers, household devices, or batteries, our team ensures every item is handled securely, separated, and logged for transparent tracking.

2. Environmentally Responsible Transportation

After collection, e-waste is transported in specially equipped vehicles to our processing hub. Here, items undergo initial sorting and packing for international shipment, adhering to strict environmental standards to minimize our carbon footprint.

3. Export to Swedish Recycling Partners

Through our exclusive partnerships with certified recyclers in Sweden, your collected electronics are shipped and tracked to their destination. Sweden‚Äôs advanced infrastructure and commitment to nearly zero landfill waste ensures each device is recycled efficiently and responsibly[2][4][14].

4. Advanced Recycling and Material Recovery

In Sweden, specialized facilities carefully disassemble, sort, and decontaminate e-waste. 5. Circular Economy & Sustainable Impact

The recycled materials are reintegrated into manufacturing chains, fostering a circular economy. This process helps reduce resource exploitation, cut greenhouse gas emissions, and protect the planet for future generations. By choosing Equail, you play a direct role in advancing global sustainability goals[4][6][14].
